# Business Case

## What This Platform Solves

The repository demonstrates how an analytics portfolio can behave like a decision product instead of a collection of notebooks.
The business problem it addresses is broader than one dataset: how to turn analytical work into reliable, governed, and executive-ready surfaces.

## Core Business Questions

- Which analytical assets are strongest enough to support executive review?
- How do quality, governance, and observability influence decision readiness?
- How can portfolio work be framed as revenue, retention, and KPI decision support instead of technical output alone?

## Executive KPIs

- `decision_readiness_score`
- `quality_pass_rate`
- `platform_trust_score`
- `governance_readiness_score`
- `observability_readiness_score`

## Business Value Created

- faster review of analytical assets
- clearer prioritization of what is production-minded versus supporting
- stronger trust in governed outputs
- more credible communication for hiring, advisory, and stakeholder review
